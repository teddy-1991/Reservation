1. Reservation Time & Usage

  • Charges are based on the time you reserved, regardless of late arrival.
  • Please begin and end your session at the scheduled time to accommodate the next guests.
  • A late check-out may result in additional charges in 10-minute increments.

2. No Outside Beverages

  • Outside beverages, including coffee, soft drinks, and alcohol, are not permitted. Only water is allowed.
  • If outside beverages are found, a $20 fee per item may be charged without prior notice, verified through CCTV.

3. Safety Notice

  • Management holds no liability for any accidents or injuries that may occur due to personal negligence within the facility. Please use the facility safely.

4. Club Rentals

  • Available rentals: 2 men’s right-handed sets, 1 men’s left-handed set, and 1 women’s right-handed set.
  • Due to limited availability, advance reservation is required.
